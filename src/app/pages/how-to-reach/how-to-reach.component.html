How to Reach Jagdish Temple

Udaipur is a well-developed and well-linked city in Rajasthan, making it easy to reach Jagdish Temple. The temple is located in the city center, close to the bus station, railway station, and airport.

1. By Personal Transport

Road trips add a new thrill to your vacation. Udaipur has a well-connected road network, offering excellent opportunities for a ride or drive with loved ones.

2. By Public Transport

Shared taxis and buses are a fun way to meet new people. Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Gujarat, and Madhya Pradesh transport corporations operate safe and affordable buses to Udaipur.

3. By Rail

The temple is located 3 km from Malvi Station in Nathdwara district. You can find direct trains to Udaipur from Delhi, Jaipur, Chittaur, Ajmer, Ahmedabad, and Jodhpur.

4. By Air

Dabok Airport is just 15 to 20 minutes away from Jagdish Temple. After deboarding, you can hire a cab for a quick journey to the temple.

5. Within the City

For commuting within Udaipur, you can hire a private taxi. Udaipur Tourism lists top car rental companies for safe and hassle-free travel to various sightseeing spots.
